All Star Games 2025

The Star Magic All-Star Games 2025 will take place on July 20, 2025, at the Smart Araneta Coliseum, featuring a mix of popular Kapamilya stars and exciting new talent. This year’s event features a thrilling mix of familiar faces and fresh talent, including Gerald Anderson, Donny Pangilinan, Angela Ken, BINI’s Mikha, BGYO’s Akira, JL, Mikki, and more. For the first time, Men’s Volleyball joins the roster, with pro athletes like Dylan Yturralde and Angel Canino adding professional firepower to the games.
